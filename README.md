# Furniture Augmented Reality (FAR)

**FAR** is a mobile app that combines machine learning and augmented reality to help users discover, preview, and choose furniture that perfectly fits their style and space.  

## VIDEO DEMO and APK (downloadable) for testing
**https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1qeDX0vJqnlhlbdxfC_cnsv3vZjiofV-p?usp=sharing**

## Features
- **User Onboarding**: Sign up/login to personalize your experience.  
- **Style Preferences**: Select 5 photos to define your furniture taste.  
- **Smart Recommendations**: ML algorithm suggests furniture tailored to your preferences.  
- **Room Measurement**: Use your phoneâ€™s camera to measure width, length, and height of your space.  
- **Augmented Reality Preview**: Place recommended furniture in your real environment using VSLAM and Unity-powered AR.  
- **History Tracking**: Save placed furniture for future browsing and comparisons.  

## How It Works
1. **Sign Up** or log in to your FAR account.  
2. **Pick 5 Style Photos** to train the recommendation system.  
3. **Measure Your Space** with your phone camera (width, length, height).  
4. **View Recommendations** tailored to your style and room size.  
5. **Place Furniture in AR** to see how it looks in your space.  
6. **Save to History** for easy access later.
